HOW TO PLAY
1. SPIN YOUR SEASON
Four majors, four randomly selected venues. Each course has unique strengths that favour different playstyles.
2. DRAFT YOUR GOLFER
For each of 6 attributes, spin to see 4 legends. Pick one — you get only their rating in that category. Build your ultimate hybrid golfer.
3. SIMULATE THE SEASON
Your golfer competes in all four majors. Course fit, random variance, and clutch performance determine the outcome.
4. CHASE THE CAREER GRAND SLAM
Win all four majors in one season to achieve the legendary Career Grand Slam. Only a handful of legends in history have ever completed it — and it's the most coveted feat in golf.
